Contribute to Our Project!

Humanity is facing new challenges in times of global warming, landscape degradation, increased social insecurity, massive migrations, and unprecedented globalization, just to name some of the factors. Consequently, scientific knowledge about this phenomenon is rapidly increasing as numerous researchers in many scientific institutions try to understand the main ways to mitigate the negative consequences of human and natural disasters. Even for the most experienced experts, navigating through the "forest" of ever-increasing publications devoted to this topic is complex. Your expertise is crucial in summarizing the knowledge about the latest scientific breakthroughs in this field. Enter the basic data about a favourite book and share your thoughts about it in the review section. Let your contribution be the starting point for a lively discussion on the topic that will affect our lives and our future, whether we like it or not.
